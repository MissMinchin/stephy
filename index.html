Studio Ghibli, officially known as Studio Ghibli, Inc.,
is a renowned Japanese animation studio based in Koganei, Tokyo.
Founded on June 15, 1985, by directors Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata,
along with producer Toshio Suzuki, Studio Ghibli has left
an indelible mark on the animation industry.
